Functional coverage models for free?

Pretty much! FunCov includes default coverage models for an array of protocols - including AHB, APB and AXI4-Stream. Over time, and through contributions from our users, the library will grow to include others.

Who is FunCov?

FunCov is a collaborative effort between engineers from AgileSoC.com and RealityReused.com. We believe there's been fun in functional coverage since before the idea even existed, but no one has noticed, until now.
