About FunCov

As much as hardware developers stress a dependency on functional coverage, usage models lag far behind the rhetoric.

FunCov is designed to address the shortcomings of collecting functional coverage. It aggregates industry knowledge of commonly used protocols in a way that is highly accessible to design and verification engineers alike. The IP you get out is exactly what you need; no less, no more.

FunCov Development Team

Neil Johnson is a verification consultant, co-moderator of AgileSoC.com and SVUnit developer. Neil hopes to improve functional coverage tools, usage models and accessibility by providing opportunities for engineers to collaboratively solve common industry problems.

Dyck Garrison, Vladimir Joliot and Glenn Wong are co-conspirators at RealityReused.com, a fictional EDA startup bent on overthrowing incumbant Big3 Inc. While FunCov is an entirely serious product, being too serious can take the fun out of the job. So for the inevitable holes, hiccups and flops during development, Dyck, Vlad and Glenn will do their best to provide a little comic relief.
