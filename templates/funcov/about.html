About FunCov

As much as hardware developers talk about our dependency on functional coverage, we believe usage lags far behind the rhetoric. Systemverilog language constructs are awkward and sporadically used by developers. That makes building functional coverage models cumbersome and error prone. Accessibility is also an issue where design engineers are completely shutout of the process.

FunCov is designed to address these shortcomings. It's a tool anyone can use, the IP you get out is exactly what you need and the code works.

FunCov Development Team

Neil Johnson is a verification consultant, co-moderator of AgileSoC.com and co-developer of SVUnit. Neil hopes to improve functional coverage tools, usage models and accessibility by providing opportunities for engineers to collaboratively solve common industry problems.

Dyck Garrison, Vladimir Joliot and Glenn Wong are co-conspirators at RealityReused.com, a fictional EDA startup bent on overthrowing incumbant Big3 Inc. While FunCov is an entirely serious product, being too serious can take the fun out of the job. So for the inevitable holes, hiccups and flops during development, Dyck, Vlad and Glenn will do their best to provide a little comic relief.
